The GCPD Crime Tracker
What

This is an individual course project. I developed an online system with Ruby on Rails that could be used by police department to track crimes. The focus on the project was to create a user-friendly interface that provided helpful feedback, prevented errors and eliminated gulfs of execution and evaluation.

Keywords

Ruby on Rails, MVC Framework, Relational Database, Entity Relationship Diagram, UI Design, Application Programming Interface

Context

Wayne Enterprises has just signed a contract with the Gotham City Police Department to build a prototype of a new online crime-tracking system and have hired you to help them deliver this new system. My job is to construct an initial version of the GCPD online crime-tracking application that will help the department track the crimes committed in the city as well as the criminals who commit them.

The Display Board of Atlantis Hub
What

This is a group project. My team was responsible for designing a data-driven display that is usable, useful, and supports decision making. The process required us to regard data as a design material which is exposed in the user interface and organized in layers of the system that are not exposed to users.

Keywords

Data, Metadata, Information Architecture, Personas, Animations, Control, Web Design

Context

The Atlantis Hub is the major transportation hub located on San Juan Island. The design of the display board at the Atlantis Hub affects the travel experiences of a large number of tourists and business travellers. We were given a sheet of unsorted data which contained detailed information of all possible routes and transportation methods of the San Juan Islands.

American Cancer Society - Cancer Survivors Network
What

This is an individual research project. I was responsible for analyzing data to compare the performance of different algorithms used by Cancer Survirvors Network and providing suggestions on UI and UX design. I accessed the database of CSN through Navicat, wrote SQL queries to analyze data, and reported the results to ACS through video chat once a week.

Keywords

SQL, Data Analysis, Navicat, Python, Machine Learning, UI/UX Design, Interview

Context

The American Cancer Society is testing a modified version of the Cancer Survivors Network (CSN) and is inviting users to try it. Participants will have access to all CSN content just as they are now. What is new is that they will see recommendations for content and other members that might interest them. These recommendations are based on what they have read on CSN. They will occasionally receive a survey to get your feedback on the new interface and may be asked about the relevance and value of the recommended content.
